

Data types in Java:
-------------------

Primary Data Types:

		Integer:
		
				byte=(2^8): -128 to 127
				short=2(2^8): 2 bytes: -32768 to 32767 
				int=4(2^8): 4 bytes
				long=8(2^8): 8 bytes
		
		floating:
				
				float=4(2^8):4 bytes
				double=8(2^8):8 bytes
		
		char: 2 bytes= 0 to 65535
		
		boolean: true/false
