

Data types in Java:
-------------------

Primary Data Types:

		Integer:
		
				byte=(2^8): -128 to 127
				short=(2^(8*2)): 2 bytes: -32768 to 32767 
				int=(2^(8*4)): 4 bytes
				long=(2^(8*8)): 8 bytes
		
		floating:
				
				float=(2^(8*4)):4 bytes
				double=(2^(8*8)):8 bytes
		
		char: 2 bytes= 0 to 65535
		
		boolean: true/false
